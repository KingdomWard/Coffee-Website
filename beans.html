Arabica


A very common type of coffee bean that is used around the world. These beans like growing in rainy places and in high altitudes.

Liberica


Native to Africa, Liberica coffee offers a unique flavor profile and is grown in many different types of climates. It also tastes more like fruit than other types of coffee beans.


Excelsa


Referred to a part of the Liberica coffee family, Excelsa is grown mainly in Asia and is very adabptable when it comes to its growing conditions. The flavor profile is slightly fruity and earthy.



Robusta


Coffee loves warmer weather and is okay with different altitudes. A very popular bean alongside Arabica and even contains more caffeine. The flavors are described as having hints of bitterness and chocolatiness.
